Humidity

As expected, the weather becomes significantly humid as one approches the equator (0 Deg Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be more humid this time of the year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
